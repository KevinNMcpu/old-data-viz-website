Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Seems like a pretty random scattering. The correlation along 0%, 20%, 40%, 75% and 100% is probably due to how OpenWeather reports a numeric value for a subjective cloud report. I'm assuming that an "Overcast" cloudiness report is in their system as "100%" while "partly cloudy" is a "40%." Some weather stations may have a more mathematically objective way of reporting how many clouds there are which is why not all weather stations are on these lines.
